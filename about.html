TANZIE MANCINI

Tanzie is a junior at Parsons School of Design, Studying studying Strategic Design and Management. She is the founder of curated vintage shop, Coco Armoire & concentrates in curated clothing collections and photography. She has over six years of experience in sustainable fashion. More specifically, sourcing and marketing curated vintage and contemporary collections. Additionally, she have one year of experience in styling, directing, and shooting campaigns for small brands. She is also skilled in social media, photography, and Adobe Suite. Her love for fashion as a form of self-expression and my enthusiasm for the fashion community drive her work. She has a strong work ethic, positive attitude, and creative approach would her a valuable addition to fashion teams.
